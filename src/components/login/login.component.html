Welcome to Git Tribe Viewer

Git Tribes Viewer is a new data visuamization concept, it's main purpose is to make the complex and boaring daskboards/interfaces more fun, by converting them to a game like ones. Our goal is to make people happy when they work, and encouraging them to do more work to impact the game/dashboard, hence improving their productivity (some sort of gamification as well).
